LET'S DO IT

Grappling Tournaments Australia organises the best competitions in the Asia-Pacific Region. We run high-stakes competitions, drawing the best grapplers and offering the best prizes that will see you competing on the world stage.

Most important of all is you – the competitor. We strive to make all of our competitions great events for you and your teammates. On and off the mat, we aim to demonstrate the very best in grappling culture.

We reward as many victors as possible with their chance to take their place among the world’s finest grapplers.
