LET'S DO IT

Grappling Tournaments Australia runs the best national competitions in the Asia-Pacific Region. We run high-stakes competitions drawing the best grapplers and winning the best prizes that will see you competing on the world stage.

Most important of all is you – the competitor. We run all of our competitions to promote a good time on and off the mat for all who compete. We reward as many victors as possible with the chance to take their place among the world’s finest grapplers.
